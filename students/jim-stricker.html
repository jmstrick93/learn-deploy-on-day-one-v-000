Biography

Jim graduated Summa Cum Laude, Phi Beta Kappa, and with Special Honors from the George Washington University in 2015 with a Bachelor of Arts in International Affairs and Middle East Studies.

Since graduating, he has experimented with multiple career paths, including corporate law, politics, and entrepreneurship. The latter resonated with him most strongly, leading him to begin learning coding and, eventually, to enroll in The Flatiron School.

Jim is proficient in Arabic and continues developing his linguistic skills in his spare time. He is also a longtime singer and musician, able to play piano, guitar, bass guitar, and banjo.
